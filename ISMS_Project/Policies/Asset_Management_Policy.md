# Asset Management Policy

## Purpose
Ensure assets are identified, inventoried, classified, and protected consistently.

## Scope
Information assets, hardware, software, documentation, and services.

## Requirements
1. Maintain an Asset Register with owner, classification, location and lifecycle.
2. Classify information into Public / Internal / Confidential / Restricted.
3. Follow secure handling, transfer and disposal procedures.
4. Label assets where required and record movements.
