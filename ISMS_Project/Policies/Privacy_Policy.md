# Privacy Policy (Data Protection)

## Purpose
Ensure personal data is processed according to applicable data protection laws and privacy principles.

## Scope
Personal Identifiable Information (PII) of employees, customers, and third parties.

## Policy
1. Data minimization and purpose limitation.
2. Implement access controls for PII.
3. Data subject rights handling process.
4. Data breach notification procedures compliant with legal timelines.

## References
- Local data protection laws
- ISO: A.5.34 (Privacy and Protection of PII)
