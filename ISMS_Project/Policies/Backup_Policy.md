# Backup Policy

## Purpose
Ensure recoverability and integrity of backups.

## Scope
All systems and data classified as business-critical.

## Requirements
1. Daily incremental backups, weekly full backups.
2. Off-site or immutable storage for backups.
3. Quarterly restore tests and annual full DR exercise.
4. Backup retention policy aligned with legal/regulatory requirements.

## Evidence
- Backup job logs
- Restore test reports
