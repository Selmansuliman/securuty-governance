# Incident Management Policy

## Purpose
Provide a coordinated approach to detect, report, respond and recover from information security incidents.

## Scope
All systems, networks, staff, third-parties, and events that could materially affect confidentiality, integrity, or availability.

## Incident Lifecycle
1. **Identification:** Detect and log incidents.
2. **Reporting:** Report to IR team within defined SLA.
3. **Containment:** Short-term containment actions to limit impact.
4. **Eradication:** Remove root cause.
5. **Recovery:** Restore services.
6. **Lessons Learned:** Post-incident review and update SoA/Risk Register.

## Roles
- **IR Lead (Responsible)**
- **CISO (Accountable)**
- **Forensics / Legal (Consulted)**
- **All Staff (Informed/Reporting)**

## Metrics & SLAs
- Mean time to detect (MTTD)
- Mean time to respond (MTTR)
- Incident severity classification and reporting timelines
