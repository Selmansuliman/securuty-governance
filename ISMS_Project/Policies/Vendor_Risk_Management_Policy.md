# Vendor Risk Management Policy

## Purpose
Manage security risks introduced by third-party suppliers.

## Scope
All third parties with access to organizational data or systems.

## Policy
1. Perform security due diligence before onboarding.
2. Include security contractual clauses and rights to audit.
3. Classify vendors by risk and apply monitoring frequency accordingly.
4. Annual reassessment and ad-hoc reviews on incidents or changes.

## Evidence
- Vendor assessment questionnaires
- Contracts with security addenda
