# Business Continuity Policy

## Purpose
Ensure continuity of critical business operations during disruptions.

## Scope
All business processes classified as critical in the BIA.

## Policy
1. Maintain Business Continuity Plan (BCP) and Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP).
2. Define Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPO).
3. Test BCP/DRP annually and after significant changes.
4. Maintain alternate facilities and backup strategies.
