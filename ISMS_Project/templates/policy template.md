# Information Security Policy

## Purpose
Define the organization's high-level commitment to information security and the ISMS objectives in line with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

## Scope
All employees, contractors, suppliers, systems, networks, applications, and information assets within the ISMS scope.

## Policy Statement
The organization will protect Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability (CIA) of information through a risk-based ISMS, continuous monitoring, and compliance with applicable legal, contractual, and regulatory obligations.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- **CISO (Accountable):** Approves policy and provides strategic direction.
- **ISMS Manager (Responsible):** Manages ISMS processes and policy lifecycle.
- **Policy Owners (Consulted):** Draft content and ensure operational fit.
- **All Staff (Informed/Responsible):** Comply with policy and report incidents.

## Key Requirements
1. Implement and maintain an ISMS aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.
2. Perform periodic risk assessments and maintain a Risk Register.
3. Implement appropriate controls selected from Annex A and justified in the SoA.
4. Ensure incident reporting, business continuity planning, and regular audits.

## Compliance & Monitoring
- Policies will be reviewed annually or after significant change.
- Non-compliance is handled under the disciplinary process and corrective action procedures.
